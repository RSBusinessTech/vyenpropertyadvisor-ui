Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) Program

The MM2H program offers a unique opportunity for foreigners to live, work, and invest in Malaysia with long-term residency benefits.

Long-Term Residency

Stay in Malaysia for up to 10 years with the option to renew your visa.

Property Ownership

Buy residential or commercial properties with fewer restrictions.

Tax Incentives

Enjoy favorable tax conditions on certain types of income.

Family Friendly

Include your spouse and dependents under one MM2H visa.

Healthcare Access

Benefit from Malaysia's quality healthcare services at affordable rates.

Education Opportunities

Enroll your children in Malaysia’s reputable schools and universities.
